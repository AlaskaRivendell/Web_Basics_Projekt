Head of Department

Famous Magizoologist Luna Lovegood has kindly agreed to lead the Department until a new official is elected in the next regular election. After her education at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry Luna spent many years traveling around the world to study all varieties of foreign and domestic magical creatures. Her books about her experience gained her well deserved respect as renowned Magizoologist. Her vast knowledge is probably only second to famous Newt Scamander.
