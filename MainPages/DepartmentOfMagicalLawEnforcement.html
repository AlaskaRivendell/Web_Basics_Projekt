Head of department

We are pleased to announced that Mr. Harry Potter has agreed to stay in as head of Department for the next term of Office. After his famous defeat of Lord Voldemort in 1998 Harry Potter proved himself to be a resourceful Auror and head of the Auror Office. In 2019 he was first appointed as Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement.
